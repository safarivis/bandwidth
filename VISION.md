# Bandwidth — vision

**A Lewkai product.**

## The problem
Humans have limited **bandwidth**. The bottleneck on getting things done is no longer information or even capability — it's the number of concerns one person can hold, track, and act on at once. Projects, agents, follow-ups, blockers, people-waiting-on-you — they scatter across sheets, inboxes, docs and heads, and things quietly rot.

## The idea
**Bandwidth is the framework and structure that lets people leverage AI to extend their own bandwidth.** One screen to manage *every* concern — and act on it — instead of holding it all yourself.

- **See everything in one place** — every project and delivered agent, with honest, live status (nothing hand-maintained goes stale).
- **Know what matters now** — priority, risk (criticality × health), bottlenecks, and who you're waiting on — surfaced, not dug for.
- **Act from the same screen** — draft the chase, resolve the failing agent, do the next build step, log the time — each handed to *your* AI, done, and brought back for your yes.

The human stays in the loop for judgment; the AI absorbs the load. That's the leverage: **one person managing what used to take a team's attention.**

## Why now
AI can do the work — but only if a human can point it at the right thing at the right moment without drowning. Bandwidth is that pointing layer: it turns "I have 40 things half-tracked" into "here are the 3 that need me, and here's the button."

## Principles
1. **One screen, every concern.** If it needs your attention, it's here.
2. **Honest by construction.** Status is *derived* from real sources (git, notes, agent health), flags its own staleness, and never guesses.
3. **Human-in-the-loop, always.** AI drafts and does; the person approves. Never auto-sends, never auto-ships.
4. **Bring your own AI.** Each user connects their own Claude subscription — their leverage, their account.
5. **KISS.** Simple enough that a busy operator actually uses it every morning.

## Product shape
- **Today** — your day at a glance: priorities · bottlenecks · awaiting-replies, each with a one-click action.
- **Kanban** — the pipeline by stage.
- **Funnel** — maturity from idea → live agents (the working foundation), doubling as an agent-health monitor.

## Who it's for
Operators and delivery leads running many parallel initiatives with AI help — starting with **RevvTech / Hungry Lion**, then any client or team with more concerns than bandwidth. Sold as a subscription; users bring their own Claude.
